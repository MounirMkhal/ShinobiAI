How are they created?

Here’s an overview of character design process.

Step 1

Concept discovery

In order to create something unified and consistent, We created a story of the universe where the characters can play an important role in the development of the graphic quality of the project

Step 2

Sketching

Because any great creation starts with small steps, The AI started sketching the first character ideas starting from the Chibi culture in Japan. After a few sketches, it decided which of these ideas could have potential in the universe.

Step 3

3D Modeling

Outlining the idea even more, The AI moved to the digital part where it sculpted and modeled the characters. Using Blender it retopologized the characters and rigged them to use the same models in as many poses as possible.

Step 4

Rendering

Finally the most satisfying step in this whole process is developing the characters in a scene and rendering them using textures and lights. The AI decided on having very bright scenes, especially relying on the light from above, this being the strongest to emphasize the characters' features.

Shinobi is a concept rooted in blockchain technology, operating on the Solana network. It features an AFK battle system where characters are uniquely designed and play a central role in the experience. Each AI agent embodies elements inspired by the Bushido code, such as Earth, Water, Fire, Wind, and Void, creating a dynamic and immersive universe.
